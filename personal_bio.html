Personal Bio

-

Passionate about data analytics, I am determined to become a Data Analyst. My journey began while working as a company secretary and administrator, where I struggled with organizing various documents and tables. Discovering the power of data analytics, I began to upskill my knowledge, self-teaching Python, SQL, and Power BI on the online platform Datacamp. With my newfound knowledge, I completed my first data analytic project.

To further solidify my determination, I applied to the Generation program, where I gained an in-depth understanding of Excel, Python, SQL, and Power BI, as well as transferable skills such as teamwork, communication, multitasking, and time management. Now, I am eager to transition into a full-time position as a Data Analyst.

My strengths, skills, and interests align with working as part of a team in a data analytic environment. As a Data Analyst, I am eager to make raw data usable for various purposes that inform and reflect the company's mission. I want to contribute data solutions and strategies for company operations and continue learning to be the best Data Analyst possible.

In summary, I am a driven and passionate individual who is eager to bring my skills and expertise to a full-time Data Analyst position. My enthusiasm for data analytics and desire to learn and grow make me a great fit for the role.
